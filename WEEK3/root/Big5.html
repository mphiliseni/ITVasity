Lion
Lions are social animals that live in groups called prides. They are apex predators and often symbolize strength and courage. Mostly found in Kruger National Park and other reserves.
Elephant
African elephants are intelligent and gentle giants, often seen in family herds. Their tusks and large ears are distinctive features. Sadly, they're under threat due to poaching.
Leopard
Leopards are solitary and stealthy cats. They're agile climbers and often drag their prey into trees to avoid scavengers. Spotting one in the wild is a rare and special event.
Rhinoceros
South Africa is home to both black and white rhinos. Their horns make them targets for poaching, but conservation efforts are ongoing to protect them.
Buffalo
African buffalo are known for their brute strength and unpredictable nature. They are herd animals and can be extremely dangerous when threatened.
